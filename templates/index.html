Annual Impact Report Generator

The purpose of this website is to generate annual impact reports based on the resident's self-assessments provided in the Care Coordination Meetings and alumni status updates given in the Graduate Follow-Up forms. The generated report will be in PDF format and will include various statistics and visualizations to showcase the impact of the program on the residents' lives.

To successfully generate a report, download the Magdalene House Dashboard spreadsheet as an Excel workbook (.xlsx) and click Choose File. From there, enter the desired year and click Generate PDF. The website will process the data and create a comprehensive report that highlights the key outcomes and achievements of the program for that year.



Please note: If there are no graduate responses from a 12-month follow-up available, the graduate outcomes section will be omitted from the report.

General Tips:

  • Ensure that the uploaded Excel workbook contains a sheet named Raw Self Assessment and another named Graduate Responses. An error message will be displayed if these sheets are missing.
  • Double-check the entered year to ensure that the data for that year is present in the workbook. The earliest year available is 2024.
  • After clicking "Generate PDF," allow some time for the processing to complete, especially if the workbook contains a large amount of data.
  • If you encounter any issues or errors during the report generation process, contact technical support for assistance.
